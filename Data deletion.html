Data Deletion Instructions

To delete your account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Open the game/app and log in to your account.
  2. Go to the settings menu.
  3. Select the option "Delete Account" or "Delete Data".
  4. Confirm your decision to delete your data.
  5. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

If you need further assistance, please contact us at gemyproduction24@gmail.com.
